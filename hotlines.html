HOTLINES:

If you need urgent medical or psychiatric attention, need help in an emergency or are in crisis:

  • Visit your local emergency department, general hospitals or call 911
  • Contact a distress centre in Ontario near you. Distress or crisis phone lines are open 24 hours a day. Crisis hotlines offer free, confidential and anonymous telephone counselling and information:
  1. Gerstein Centre- 416-929-5200, 24 hrs/7 days
  2. Spectra Helpline: 416 920-0497 or 905 459-7777 for Brampton and Mississauga residents
  3. TTY: 905 278-4890; Languages: English, Punjabi, Hindi, Urdu, Spanish, Portuguese
  4. Durham Crisis and Mental Health Line: 905 666-0483
  5. Good2Talk (Anonymous helpline for post-secondary students) - 1-866-925-5454, 24 hrs/7 days

Support/Self-Help Group:

  • Canadian Mental Health Association 416-789-7957 (in Toronto)
